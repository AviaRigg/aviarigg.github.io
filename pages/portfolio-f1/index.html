SF-25 Livery

A production-ready hard surface rig for the Ferrari SF-25 F1 car. Built entirely in Maya 2022+ with a clean, animator-friendly hierarchy. Features auto-spinning wheels driven by motion, a DRS rear wing system, dual livery switching between Hamilton and Leclerc via custom attribute, and full AnimSchool Picker support.

// Technical Notes
01
Auto Wheel Spin
Wheels spin automatically based on world movement via expression — no manual keyframing needed. Override available for custom rotation.
02
DRS System
Rear wing elements are driven by a single DRS attribute. Open/close is fully animatable with clean deformation and no geometry intersection.
03
Dual Livery
Hamilton and Leclerc liveries switchable via a custom attribute on the main controller. No shader reassignment or manual toggling required.
04
Hierarchy
Fully organized outliner with logical grouping. Root_Scale at the top for global positioning. All controls follow AviaRigg naming conventions.
